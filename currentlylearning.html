bio

Feel free to explore my work here—I’m a front-end developer focused on creating clean, intuitive systems that are a pleasure to use. I also take on freelance projects, so if you’re interested in collaborating, don’t hesitate to reach out through this website. Outside of work, I’m a 37-year-old Dutch creative learning Japanese (around N4) and planning to study in Japan. In my spare time, I’m restoring a kei van, blending hands-on work with a passion for slow progress and practical design. I also share some music on Bandcamp—feel free to check it out if you’re into that.
